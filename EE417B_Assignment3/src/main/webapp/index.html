LCR the perfect rental company for luxury cars

Welcome to your new luxury car rental company. Although we are new in the field, our garage has the most beautiful cars in the world. You will find on this site a specific sheet for each vehicle. You can first learn more about the history of the brand via a YouTube video presenting it. To give you a first impression of each vehicle we have, this sheet is completed by a link to a video presenting a test drive of the vehicle.

All you have to do is make your choice! What could be better than to drive the most prestigious cars in the world, thanks to the experience we offer you. Brands such as Bugatti, Porsche but also Ferrari or Lamborghini become available for rent. Each vehicle can be rented for different purposes. You can rent it by the day, by the week or, for any motorsport fan, you can rent it exclusively to drive on a circuit. If you are not a fan of motor sports, no problem! We also have cars that allow you to enjoy the wind in your hair on the French Riviera.
